Our Specialist Fencing Types

We specialize in three main fencing styles designed to withstand the Brisbane climate while adding maximum value to your home.

Colorbond ® Steel Fencing

Colorbond is the gold standard for Australian backyards. It offers 100% privacy, is fire-resistant, and won't rot or be eaten by termites. Available in 14 different colors, it provides a sleek, modern finish that never needs painting.

  • Low maintenance
  • Termite and rot proof
  • Available in modern designer colors
  • Includes 10-year manufacturer warranty

Quality Timber Fencing

Nothing beats the natural warmth of real timber. We use high-quality, CCA-treated hardwood or pine to ensure your timber fence resists the elements. From standard lap-and-cap to decorative picket styles, we build it right.

  • Natural aesthetic looks great in any garden
  • Treated timber for long-term durability
  • Custom heights and styles available
  • Most cost-effective privacy solution

Brick & Masonry Fences

For the ultimate in security and sound insulation, brick fencing is the premium choice. Perfect for homes on busy roads or for creating a stately entrance. We handle everything from the concrete footings to the final bricklaying and rendering.

  • Unmatched sound insulation
  • Maximum property security
  • Increases significant curb appeal
  • Can be combined with timber or steel inserts
