Our Specialist Fencing Types 🏗️

We specialize in three main fencing styles designed to withstand the Brisbane climate while adding maximum value to your home.

Colorbond ® Steel Fencing 🛡️

Colorbond is the gold standard for Australian backyards. It offers 100% privacy, is fire-resistant, and won't rot or be eaten by termites.

  • Low maintenance ✨
  • Termite and rot proof 🐜
  • Modern designer colors 🎨
  • 10-year warranty 📜

Quality Timber Fencing 🪵

Nothing beats the natural warmth of real timber. We use high-quality, CCA-treated hardwood or pine to ensure your timber fence resists the elements.

  • Natural aesthetic 🌿
  • Treated for durability 🌧️
  • Custom heights available 📏
  • Cost-effective solution 💰

Brick & Masonry Fences 🧱

For the ultimate in security and sound insulation, brick fencing is the premium choice. Perfect for busy roads or creating a stately entrance.

  • Sound insulation 🔇
  • Maximum security 🔒
  • Curb appeal 💎
  • Hybrid styles available 🤝
